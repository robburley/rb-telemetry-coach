# Privacy Policy for RB Telemetry Coach

Effective date: [DATE]

RB Telemetry Coach is a browser extension developed by Rob Burley. This privacy policy explains what information the extension can access, how that information is used, and what information is collected or shared.

## Summary

RB Telemetry Coach runs locally in your browser. It does not send telemetry, lap data, driver names, Garage 61 URLs, usage data, analytics, error logs, or any other data to the developer or to third-party services.

The extension reads data that is already loaded by the active Garage 61 page, performs calculations in the browser, applies local analysis rules, and displays coaching findings to you.

## What the extension does

RB Telemetry Coach adds a coaching panel to Garage 61 analysis pages. When you are viewing a Garage 61 lap analysis with two active laps, the extension can analyse the currently zoomed slice of the lap and provide coaching-style feedback based on available telemetry such as speed, braking, throttle, steering, gear, RPM, and racing line information.

The extension works only on Garage 61 pages.

## Information the extension can access

When you use the extension on a Garage 61 analysis page, it may observe Garage 61 page data that is already loaded in your browser, including:

* lap comparison metadata
* lap telemetry
* driver names shown in the active comparison
* lap times and sector times
* car and track information
* the currently selected or zoomed distance range
* telemetry traces such as speed, brake, throttle, steering, gear, RPM, and location/path data where available

The extension uses this information only to generate local coaching findings inside the browser.

## Information the extension does not collect

RB Telemetry Coach does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or store:

* telemetry data
* lap metadata
* driver names
* Garage 61 account details
* Garage 61 cookies
* authentication tokens
* browsing history
* analytics events
* crash reports
* error logs
* usage statistics
* personal information

## Local processing only

All analysis is performed locally in your browser. The extension reads available Garage 61 page data, performs calculations, applies deterministic coaching rules, and displays the result in the extension panel.

No data is sent to external servers.

## Third-party services

RB Telemetry Coach does not use third-party analytics, monitoring, logging, artificial intelligence APIs, remote configuration services, databases, or backend servers.

In particular, the extension does not use services such as Google Analytics, Sentry, PostHog, Firebase, Supabase, OpenAI, or any developer-controlled server.

## Local storage

RB Telemetry Coach does not persistently store telemetry, reports, preferences, lap IDs, debug logs, or user data.

The extension may temporarily hold observed Garage 61 data in memory while the active page is open so it can calculate and display coaching findings. This in-memory data is discarded when the page is closed, refreshed, or the extension context is reset.

## Garage 61 account data

RB Telemetry Coach does not directly read your Garage 61 cookies, authentication tokens, localStorage, sessionStorage, password, or account settings.

The extension observes data that the active Garage 61 page has already loaded and made available in the browser for the current analysis view.

## Other drivers’ telemetry

Garage 61 may allow users to compare laps from different drivers depending on Garage 61’s own sharing and privacy settings. RB Telemetry Coach analyses the two active laps currently selected in the Garage 61 analysis page, which may include another driver’s lap if that lap is available to you in Garage 61.

The extension does not change Garage 61 privacy settings, bypass Garage 61 permissions, or redistribute any lap data.

## Data sharing

RB Telemetry Coach does not share data with the developer, Garage 61, third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

## Independent extension

RB Telemetry Coach is an independent browser extension. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to Garage 61 or Rocketeer Comm. V.

Garage 61 is a separate service with its own terms and privacy policy.

## Children’s privacy

RB Telemetry Coach does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If the extension’s data practices change materially, the policy will be updated before or at the time the change is released.
